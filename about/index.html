BE.

BE. is a design agency based in Florence, founded by Barbara Benini and Eleonora Franchi.

Our focus is interior design and styling, product design, art and research. Our approach is conceptual and eclectic while the feeling and the attention to detail are the determining factor of our work.

We are attracted by the emotional side of design in a way that brings an expressive and seductive power to spaces. We focus on the mood and the connections among their pulsing creative energy.
