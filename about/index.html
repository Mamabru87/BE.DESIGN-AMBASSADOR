About BE.DESIGN AMBASSADOR

BE.DESIGN AMBASSADOR is an international design studio founded by Arianna Lelli Mami and Chiara Di Pinto.

The studio is renowned for its distinctive perspective and architectural language, balancing formal rigor with poetic vision across multiple disciplines including residential design, retail spaces, hospitality projects, and product design.

Based in Milan, BE.DESIGN AMBASSADOR works on projects spanning continents, creating authorial and multi-referential design solutions that have gained international recognition.
