## ABOUT

---

### ABOUT / Studio

BE. DESIGN AMBASSADOR is a design agency based in Florence, founded by Barbara Benini and Eleonora Franchi.

The studio operates across interior design and styling, product design, art and research.

Its approach is conceptual and eclectic, defined by sensitivity and attention to detail.

#### Founders

**Barbara Benini**

**Eleonora Franchi**

#### Base

Florence, Italy

---

### ABOUT / Design Approach

The studio is drawn to the emotional dimension of design.

This is understood as the expressive and seductive power of spaces.

The work focuses on mood and on the connections between the creative energies of environments.

#### Defining Elements

Sensitivity.  
Attention to detail.  
Conceptual thinking.  
Eclectic vision.

## SERVICES

---

### SERVICES / Interiors

Interior design for residential spaces.

The studio works on apartments in historic buildings and ancient palazzi, early 20th-century townhouses and villas, country houses.

Projects range from complete interior renovations to heritage preservation combined with contemporary design language.

#### Scope

Residential interiors in historic buildings  
Complete interior renovation  
Heritage preservation  
Contemporary design language

#### Materials and Finishes

Lime plaster  
Lime wash finishes  
Plaster moldings and frames  
Brass  
Marble  
Lacquered wood

#### Deliverables

Technical drawings  
Elevations  
Construction details

---

### SERVICES / Styling

Styling as an extension of interior design.

The studio develops layered and stratified compositions, with attention to mood and the connections between creative energies within environments.

---

### SERVICES / Product Design

## PROCESS

The studio's work develops through a defined sequence of phases.

### Phase 01 — Concept

Understanding the context.  
Defining mood and spatial intention.  
Conceptual direction.

### Phase 02 — Design Development

Technical drawings.  
Elevations.  
Material and finish selection.  
Custom furniture design.

### Phase 03 — Detailing

Construction details.  
Artisanal coordination.  
Production specifications.

Site supervision.  
Execution control.  
Final styling.
